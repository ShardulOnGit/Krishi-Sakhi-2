# 🌾 Krishi Sakhi – Smart Farming Assistance Website

Krishi Sakhi is a **voice-first, multilingual, farmer-focused web application** designed to help farmers take **timely, confident, and informed farming decisions**.

The platform combines **verified knowledge**, **community experience**, and **optional web-based information**, ensuring farmers never have to wait for answers.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Farmers take important daily decisions related to irrigation, crop care, disease management, machinery usage, and market access.  
However, they face several challenges:

- Information is scattered and confusing  
- Many platforms are English-only and text-heavy  
- Community answers take time to arrive  
- Disease diagnosis through apps can be risky or inaccurate  
- Access to machinery, animals, and markets depends on middlemen  

The core problem is the **lack of simple, accessible, and timely guidance designed for farmers**.

---

## 💡 Solution

Krishi Sakhi acts as a **digital companion for farmers** by providing:

- Voice-based interaction instead of typing  
- Multilingual support for local accessibility  
- Instant answers through verified knowledge  
- Community discussion for shared experience  
- Optional web search for on-demand information  
- Safe and guided disease checking  
- Local discovery-based marketplaces  

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🗣️ Voice-First Interaction
- Ask questions using voice
- Get answers in voice and text
- Designed for low digital literacy users

---

### 🌍 Multilingual Support
- English
- Hindi
- Marathi
- Easy language switching

---

### 🧠 Smart Community System
A three-layer answer model:
1. **Pre-Answered Verified Questions** (Instant help)
2. **Community Discussion** (Farmer experience)
3. **Optional Web Search** (User-triggered, clearly labeled)

All answer types are **clearly separated for trust**.

---

### 🌿 Disease Check (Improved & Safe Design)
Instead of risky one-click diagnosis, Krishi Sakhi uses a **guided disease check system**:

- Image upload + simple symptom questions
- Shows **possible causes**, not a single guaranteed disease
- Confidence levels (High / Medium / Needs expert)
- Focus on **safe first actions** before treatments
- Option to ask community or consult expert
- Clear disclaimer to avoid misuse

This approach reduces risk and builds farmer trust.

---

### 🐄 Farm Animal Buy–Sell Marketplace
- Discovery and connection only
- No payments or logistics handled
- Allowed animals: Cow, Buffalo, Goat
- Trust badges and clear disclaimers
- Location-based discovery

---

### 🚜 Farm Machinery Rent & Sell
- Rent or sell machines locally
- No platform involvement in payment or delivery
- Reduces cost of farming equipment
- Simple listing and contact flow

---

### 📋 Daily Farming Tasks & Reminders
- Crop-based daily task suggestions
- Simple checklists
- Helps farmers stay on schedule
